## Project Title

**Quantum Density Matrix Monte Carlo Benchmarking Tool**

## Technical Innovation

4D Hilbert-space simulator with:
- SU(4) unitary dynamics
- 1M-sample POVM benchmarking
- Formal Lean 4 verification

### Metrics

- **Crit-rate fidelity**: Binary outcome classification accuracy
- **Shannon/Von Neumann entropy**: Measurement vs. state uncertainty
- **Relativistic energy expectation**: E=mc² observables
- **Purity**: Tr(ρ²) mixed-state characterization

## Stage A Deliverable ($1M Request)

### Year 1 Objectives

1. **Benchmark 15 QBI algorithms** against classical Monte Carlo baselines
   - Compare quantum advantage margins
   - Identify noise-resilience thresholds
   - Validate NISQ-era performance

2. **Formal Lean 4 verification** of density matrix axioms
   - Hermiticity, positive semi-definiteness, trace normalization
   - Born rule probability guarantees
   - CPTP (Completely Positive Trace-Preserving) map proofs

3. **Cancer topology application**
   - Persistent homology on mixed tumor states
   - Treatment response modeling via density-matrix evolution
   - Collaboration with NIH/NCI cancer centers

### Deliverables

- **Software**: Open-source GitHub repository with Docker containers
- **Publications**: 2 peer-reviewed papers (arXiv preprints)
- **Benchmarks**: Public dataset of 15 algorithm comparisons
- **Proofs**: Lean 4 formalization contributed to mathlib4
